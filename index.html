Cheng Chen

I am a third-year Ph.D. student at the Elmore Family School of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Purdue University, supervised by Prof. Saurabh Bagchi and Prof. Kwang Taik Kim. Before that, I obtained a Bachelor's degree at Nanjing University in 2020 and a Master's degree at New York University in 2022. I spent the Spring semester at University of California, Berkeley as an exchange student in 2019. My research focuses on intelligent computer vision systems. Currently, I am improving the quality, robustness, and efficiency of cooperative perception for connected autonomous vehicles (CAVs). Previously, I worked on optimizing ensemble inference across heterogeneous edge devices to be deadline-aware and energy-efficient.
